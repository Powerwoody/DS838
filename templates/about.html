About Us

Welcome to your new everyday food!

We created this platform with one goal in mind: to make everyday meals easier, smarter, and more inspiring – without compromising your values or your wallet. Using AI-generated recipes and ideas based on Netto’s product catalog, we help you find delicious and easy dishes that match exactly what you care about. Choose from three focus areas:

  • Budget Friendly – Flavorful dishes that stretch your budget further
  • Organic – Recipes focused on sustainability and clean ingredients
  • European produced – Meal ideas based on products with European ingredients

Food shouldn’t be a burden – it should be a pleasure!
